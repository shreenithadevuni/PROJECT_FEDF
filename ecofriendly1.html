// The Process

How EcoMark Works

A transparent, criteria-driven scoring engine that brings honesty to every product label.

01
🔬
Analyse Product

We examine material composition, recyclability, energy usage, packaging impact, and supply chain carbon.

02
📊
Calculate Eco-Score

A weighted algorithm combines each sustainability criterion into a transparent 0–100 eco-score.

03
🏅
Assign Badge

Products earn Platinum, Gold, Silver, or Bronze badges displayed directly on the product listing.

04
🎁
Reward Shoppers

Users accumulate green points and discounts every time they choose eco-certified products.

// Certification Tiers

The Four Eco-Badge Levels

Scores range from 0–100. Higher scores unlock better badges and exclusive green rewards.

🏆
Platinum
Score: 85 – 100

Exceptional sustainability across all criteria. Industry-leading green practices.

🥇
Gold
Score: 70 – 84

Strong eco-performance with minimal environmental impact across production.

🥈
Silver
Score: 50 – 69

Above-average sustainability with ongoing improvements in key areas.

🥉
Bronze
Score: 30 – 49

Beginning the sustainability journey with measurable positive actions.
